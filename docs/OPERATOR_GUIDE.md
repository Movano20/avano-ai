# Avano AI — Operator Quick Guide

## What Avano AI Does For You

Once connected, Avano AI handles your entire customer interaction flow automatically:

1. **Customer contacts you** via WhatsApp or Telegram
2. **AI agent responds** instantly in the customer's language
3. **Booking is created** — vehicle, dates, customer details, all captured automatically
4. **Contract is generated** and sent to the customer
5. **Dashboard updates** in real-time — you see everything

You focus on running your fleet. Avano handles the conversations.

---

## Operator Dashboard

Access your dashboard at `app.avanoai.ai`

### Fleet View
- See all vehicles: Available, Rented, Under Maintenance
- Click any status to filter and sort
- Update vehicle status with one click

### Live Operations
- See every active and upcoming booking in real-time
- Click any booking to view full details and conversation history
- **Take Over** any conversation to speak directly with the customer
- **End Takeover** to hand back to the AI agent

### Customers
- Full customer profiles with contact history
- Trust scoring based on booking history and network data
- Blacklist management shared across the operator network
